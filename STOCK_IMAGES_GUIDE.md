## Free Stock Photo Resources

Here are excellent free stock photo websites where you can download professional dental and medical images:

### **Top Recommended Sites** (No signup needed)

1. **Unsplash** - https://unsplash.com
   - Search: "dentist", "dental office", "teeth whitening", "orthodontist"
   - High quality, completely free
   - No credit required

2. **Pexels** - https://www.pexels.com
   - Search: "dental care", "medical professional", "teeth cleaning"
   - Free to use and modify
   - Great quality photos

3. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com
   - Search: "dentist office", "dental treatment", "smile"
   - Free commercial use
   - Over 3 million images

4. **Freepik** - https://www.freepik.com (free tier available)
   - Search: "dental clinic", "dentist professional", "patient care"
   - Both photos and illustrations
   - Free account available

2. Search for relevant dental images
3. Download the image (usually available in multiple sizes)
4. Choose medium-high resolution (1200x800px or similar)

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Image Quality**: Choose high-resolution images (at least 600x400px)
2. **Consistency**: Keep a consistent style (either all professional photos OR all illustrations)
3. **Optimization**: Compress images before adding (use online tools like TinyPNG)
4. **Naming**: Use clear, descriptive file names for easy identification
5. **Alt Text**: Always include descriptive alt text for accessibility
