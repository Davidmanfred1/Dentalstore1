Our Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care for the whole family

Preventive Care

Regular Checkups & Cleanings

Routine dental examinations and professional cleanings help detect and prevent dental problems early. We recommend visits every 6 months for optimal oral health.

Starting at: $100

Dental X-Rays

Digital radiography provides detailed images of your teeth and jaw structure. Early detection of cavities, bone loss, and other issues is crucial for effective treatment.

Starting at: $50

Fluoride Treatments

Professional fluoride application strengthens tooth enamel and helps prevent decay. Especially beneficial for children and patients prone to cavities.

Starting at: $30

Sealants

Protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth to prevent decay. Ideal for children and cavity-prone patients.

Starting at: $40 per tooth

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings

We offer tooth-colored composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth. Durable and aesthetic restoration for cavities.

Starting at: $150

Crowns & Bridges

Custom-designed ceramic crowns restore damaged teeth, while bridges replace missing teeth. Long-lasting and natural-looking solutions.

Starting at: $800

Root Canal Therapy

Save your natural tooth with endodontic treatment. Our experienced dentists use modern techniques to remove infected pulp and seal the tooth.

Starting at: $600

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with durable implants that look and function like natural teeth. A permanent solution for tooth loss.

Starting at: $1,500

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments brighten your smile effectively and safely. Choose between in-office treatments or take-home kits.

Starting at: $200

Veneers

Thin porcelain shells cover the front surface of teeth to improve appearance. Perfect for discoloration, chips, or gaps.

Starting at: $900 per tooth

Bonding

Tooth-colored resin material reshapes and improves your smile. A quick and affordable cosmetic solution.

Starting at: $200

Smile Makeover

Combine multiple treatments to achieve your dream smile. We create a customized plan tailored to your goals.
