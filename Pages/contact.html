Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our booking system, call us at (234) 567-8900, or visit our office in person.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Contact us to verify if your insurance is accepted.

Do you offer emergency care?

Yes! We provide same-day emergency appointments for dental pain and urgent issues. Call us immediately for assistance.

Are your dentists experienced?

All our dentists are licensed and board-certified with extensive experience in their specialties.

Do you offer sedation options?

Yes, we offer local anesthesia and sedation options to ensure your comfort during procedures.

Can I pay with a payment plan?

We offer flexible payment plans and work with several financing options to make care affordable.
